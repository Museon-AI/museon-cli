# Code of Conduct

Museon wants this project to be a useful, welcoming place to collaborate.

Be respectful, assume good intent, and focus feedback on the work. Harassment,
discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, and the publication of another
person's private information are not acceptable in project spaces.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when
needed to protect the community. Report conduct concerns privately to a Museon
organization owner. Reports will be reviewed discreetly and handled as promptly
and fairly as possible.
