# Security policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please do not open a public issue for a suspected vulnerability, leaked
credential, authorization bypass, or customer-data exposure.

Use the repository's **Security** tab and choose **Report a vulnerability** to
send the maintainers a private report. Include affected versions, reproduction
steps, impact, and any suggested mitigation. If private vulnerability reporting
is not yet available, contact a Museon organization owner privately.

The maintainers will acknowledge a complete report, investigate it, and
coordinate disclosure after affected users have a reasonable opportunity to
update.
